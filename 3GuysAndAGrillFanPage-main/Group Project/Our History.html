OUR HISTORY

Three Guys And A Grill is a cozy restaurant owned by three friends from different parts of the world. Duncan, a Kiwi; Tom, who hails from Switzerland; and Frank, a Filipino-American, came together to share their homemade recipes with locals and foreigners alike. Their journey began with a shared desire to introduce the best sausages to the Philippines.

Inside the warm and inviting restaurant, guests can witness the trusty grill where all the magic happens. Using locally sourced ingredients, they craft mouth-watering dishes and sauces in their kitchen. Their homemade ketchup, muscovado sugar, and fruity guava mustard add unique flavors to every bite.

Concerned about health, Three Guys And A Grill offers homemade sodas infused with fruit-flavored syrup as an alternative to soft drinks. They aim to provide a wholesome dining experience for everyone.

Known for their expertise in hardwood charcoal grilling, Three Guys And A Grill operates as a supper club and catering service in Sheboygan County's Broughton Marsh Park. The rustic log cabin ambiance adds charm to the dining experience, perfect for families.

Their menu features tempting appetizers like alligator, deep-fried asparagus, and Wisconsin's staple white and orange cheddar cheese curds. Guests can savor delicious charcoal-grilled dinner sandwiches and indulge in hand-muddled Old-Fashioned cocktails at the full bar.

On weekends, guests can enjoy breakfast while taking in the recreational activities offered at the Marsh Park, including an observation tower, canoeing, fishing, playground, and disk and foot-golf courses.

Three Guys & a Grill offers fantastic charcoal grill catering services for those planning events. With portable grills on wheels, they can bring the excitement of hardwood charcoal cooking to any party within a 100-mile radius, serving over 1000 people with a wide selection of menu options.
